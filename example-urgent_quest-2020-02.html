CBT: Urgent Quests Schedule

Don’t think this Closed Beta Test will be a leisurely walk in the Shopping Plaza. The Falspawn wait for no one. Prepare for Urgent Quests!

Prepare yourself, Arks, because Urgent Quests are coming! During these challenging missions, you’ll join forces with up to eleven other Arks to wage war against the most formidable of the Falspawn. With warning sirens blaring and loudspeaker announcements, you’ll know that an Urgent Quest is imminent. Make the necessary preparations and promptly make your way to the Quest Counter to take part in these epic battles!

Urgent Quest “Cradle of Darkness”

During the analysis of an unidentified ARKS signal, personnel has determined the location of an apparent Falspawn nest. This is an opportunity to eradicate the enemy at one of the sources, so operatives are strongly encouraged to report for duty. Falspawn are likely to be numerous, so Arks should equip themselves accordingly. 

 


Urgent Quest “Giant Automata: The Awakening”

ARKS Personnel has picked up on a massive, moving heat signature radiating from underground on Lillipa. Initial thoughts are that a dreadnought-class Automata has awakened in response to unknown stimuli. The potential for researcher casualties and damage to the ancient structures is great, therefore immediate interception of this Automata is required. Operatives are to report to the Quest Counter for immediate deployment to the area.

 


Urgent Quest “The Raging Obsidian Arm”

Attention Arks! We appear to be under a blitz attack from a fragment of Elder the Gargantuan that is approaching at great speed. Initial analysis indicates that this fragment is atypical of those previously encountered, therefore Arks are to quickly arm themselves for a tough fight. Your mission is to intercept and weaken the fragment, in addition to delaying it from making contact with the Fleet until full-scale resources can be deployed.

 


Sequel Urgent Quest “Elder of the Unfathomable Abyss”

Earlier attempts to weaken the fragment of Elder the Gargantuan appear to have only enraged it, and all Arks are required to prepare for what could be Fleet Oracle's final battle. However, operatives are to remember that their combined effort and passion cannot but lead to victory. Take comfort in the strength of your comrades and prepare for the battle to come.

 

- The PSO2 Team
