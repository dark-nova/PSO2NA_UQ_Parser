UQs/Concerts: May 2020 - Part 3

Get ready to get drenched, Arks! For the next few weeks, you will be combating the elements in addition to Falspawn! Read this article to learn more about upcoming Urgent Quests!

Greetings, Arks! Fighting conditions are going to be less than ideal in the next few weeks, as the Forest has been experiencing torrential downpours lately in addition to abnormal F-factor readings. Furthermore, Harukotan has been exhibiting rising levels of F-factor as well. The next two weeks are going to be busy, operatives, so buckle up for Urgent Quests! More information about Urgent Quests can be found here.

Quna has greatly enjoyed her time off and is ready to enthrall more Arks in the Shopping Plaza during one of her live concerts! These live events tend to have a lasting effect on operatives and impart good luck to Arks who engage in battle shortly thereafter. Operatives have reported finding more Meseta and rare items and feel as though their learning ability is heightened. For these benefits, in addition to the amazing performances, these events are not to be missed!

May 2020: Part 3

The excitement continues with Part 3 of May! Prepare yourselves for the battles below and take heart in the continuation of Quna’s performances!

Mining Base Defense Operations: Despair

Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable: Players must protect base sites from eight enemy waves.)

The urgent attention of all ARKS Operatives is required! There are several strong Falspawn signatures converging on the Lillipan Mining Base we have recently been defending. These reinforcements may seem to present ARKS with potential defeat, but fear not, for we have a newly developed Site Defense System ready for deployment. Climb into your AIS, Arks, and prepare for battle!




The Raging Obsidian Arm

Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable: each run must be completed within 20 minutes.)

Attention Arks! We appear to be under a blitz attack from a fragment of Elder the Gargantuan that is approaching at great speed. Initial analysis indicates that this fragment is atypical of those previously encountered. All Arks are to quickly arm themselves for a much tougher fight with the goal of intercepting and weakening the fragment to provide time for full-scale resources to be deployed.




Elder of the Unfathomable Abyss (Sequel)

Duration: 30 Minutes. (Players must complete “The Raging Obsidian Arm” to gain access to this Urgent Quest.)

Earlier attempts to weaken the fragment of Elder the Gargantuan appear to have only strengthened it, and all Arks are required to prepare for what could be the final battle for ARKS. However, operatives are encouraged to remember that their combined effort and passion cannot but lead to victory. Take comfort in the strength of your comrades and prepare for the battle to come.





Mining Base Defense Operations: Incursion

Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable: Players must protect base sites from six enemy waves.)

The urgent attention of all ARKS Operatives is needed! Falspawn activity has been detected on the Mining Base of Lilipa. Expect waves of Falspawn as they attempt to gain a foothold on the base so as to procure potentially valuable resources. Defense of this base is paramount for Fleet Oracle, and all operatives are to prepare to mount a fierce defense of the area. Equip yourselves for a sustained effort, Arks!


 

 

Gone With the Wind & Rain

Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable)

In addition to abnormal weather, the natives of the Forest on planet Naverius are exhibiting abnormal behavior. Current hypotheses state that their divergent actions are due to an elevated level of F-factor, but operatives are needed to gather additional data in the effort to reach a conclusion. A large form has also been glimpsed from afar, and its intentions are unknown at this point. If encountered, operatives are to approach the entity with the utmost caution and determine its threat prior to making attempts at neutralization. Arks are to ensure their gear is water-repellant and stand by for deployment when directed to do so by intercom communications.


 


Drawn to Darkness

Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable.)

ARKS intelligence has picked up on strong Falspawn readings originating from the Corrupted Mothership. Either Dark Falz or another powerful entity must be responsible for these readings, based on their amplitude. In addition to this centralized spike of activity, the presence of numerous other F-factor signals have been detected.  The Council cautions that you proceed with caution as you move through the Mothership and eliminate all hostiles onboard.


The Cunning Black Winged Vanguard

Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable: each run must be completed within 20 minutes.)

Your immediate attention is needed, Arks! The Mothership has been corrupted and is moving to intercept the main fleet of Oracle. As this course runs countermand to previous navigational orders, it is believed that the helm has been taken over by Dark Falz and his minions. Operatives are to prepare for an all-out attempt to retake what is ours, eradicate all Falspawn, and locate Dark Falz.

Fleet Oracle is blessed with the bravery and determination of ARKS Operatives, and the Falspawn shall tremble before their combined might! See you in the Lobby, operatives!




The False Champion: Luther the Fallen (Sequel)

Duration: 30 Minutes. (Players must complete "The Cunning Black Winged Vanguard" to gain access to this Urgent Quest.)

The readings of corruption from the Mothership have increased in amplitude, indicating that Dark Falz Luther is aboard. He is familiar with the layout of the Ship, so Arks should be wary of traps and ambushing Falspawn. The outcome of the final battle is in the hands of our brave operatives, and we have no doubt they shall triumph.



Fleet Oracle is blessed with the bravery and determination of ARKS Operatives, and the Falspawn shall tremble before their combined might! See you in the Lobby, operatives!
