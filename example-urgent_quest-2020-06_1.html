UQs/Concerts: June 2020 - Part 1

The storm continues in PSO2! In addition to the Urgent Quest “Gone with the Wind and the Rain,” there will be a few new Urgent Quests in the mix for Part 1 of June. Read this article to learn more!

Greetings Arks! The month of June has arrived, and though blessings may shower those in Fleet Oracle during the Wedding Event, the Falspawn will not be held at bay by these pleasant tidings. Dark Falz Luther still clamors for control of the Mothership, unusual weather patterns still plague the Forest of Naverius, and the Falspawn once more seek to breach our hulls. In addition, the planet Harukotan stirs; ARKS HQ has reported that the F-factor readings emanating from the planet show a strange, undulating pattern. It is theorized that some large entity must be responsible, and all operatives will be needed in the coming weeks to withstand its suspected power. As forewarned is forearmed, if you have questions about these upcoming Urgent Quests it is in your best interest to learn more about them here.

 On a lighter note, Quna is ready to grace us with some new songs in her live concerts! You may also see Hatsune Miku and Megurine Luka popping up from time to time as well, singing their hit “Living Universe.” These live performances tend to have a lasting effect on operatives and impart good luck to Arks who engage in battle shortly thereafter. Operatives have reported finding more Meseta and rare items and feel as though their learning ability is heightened. These benefits, in addition to stellar choreography and lighting, make these live performances a “must-see”!

*In addition to the Urgent Quests listed in the schedule above, ARKS Operatives will also be able to face several Urgent Quests at random times throughout the day.



The Cunning Black Winged Vanguard


Duration: 20 Minutes (Repeatable: each run must be completed within 20 minutes)


Your immediate attention is needed, Arks! The Mothership has been corrupted and is moving to intercept the main fleet of Oracle. As this course runs countermand to previous navigational orders, it is believed that the helm has been taken over by Dark Falz and his minions. Operatives are to prepare for an all-out attempt to retake what is ours, eradicate all Falspawn, and locate Dark Falz.



The False Champion: Luther the Fallen (Sequel)


Duration: 30 Minutes (Players must complete "The Cunning Black Winged Vanguard" to gain access to this Urgent Quest)


The readings of corruption from the Mothership have increased in amplitude, indicating that Dark Falz Luther is aboard. He is familiar with the layout of the Ship, so Arks should be wary of traps and ambushing Falspawn. The outcome of the final battle is in the hands of our brave operatives, and we have no doubt they shall triumph.



Drawn to Darkness


Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable)


ARKS HQ has picked up on strong Falspawn readings originating from the Corrupted Mothership. Either Dark Falz or another powerful entity must be responsible for these readings, based on their amplitude. In addition to this centralized spike of activity, the presence of numerous other F-factor signals have been detected.  ARKS HQ urges operatives to proceed with caution as they move through the Mothership and eliminate all hostiles onboard.



The Manifested Planetbreaker


Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable, up to 4 times)


ARKS HQ has pin-pointed abnormal readings originating from the planet Harukotan. The Harbinger of Calamity has assumed a new form, and should it succeed in breaching the defensive barrier…well, suffice it to say that the magnitude of the resulting devastation is unfathomable. Fleet Oracle will need every valiant ARKS Operative to suit up and ship out with all due haste to neutralize this threat. Operatives are encouraged to check their supplies of recovery items before boarding a Gateway Ship.



The Chant to Cleanse the Calamity (Sequel)


Duration: 7 Minutes (Players must complete “The Manifested Planetbreaker” to gain access to this Urgent Quest)


Sukunahime has partially succeeded in her effort to save Harukotan by entrapping a part of the Harbinger of Calamity within the Endsong Realm. This part of the Harbinger must be kept occupied while its spirit is purified during the imprisonment. To aid Arks in diverting the Harbinger from focusing on escape, modified AIS units with a limited range will be provided. The cooperation of all Arks is needed to neutralize this dangerous manifestation.



Gone With the Wind & Rain


Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable)


In addition to abnormal weather, the natives of the Forest on planet Naverius are exhibiting abnormal behavior. Current hypotheses state that their divergent actions are due to an elevated level of F-factor, but operatives are needed to gather additional data in the effort to reach a conclusion. A large form has also been glimpsed from afar, and its intentions are unknown at this point. If encountered, operatives are to approach the entity with the utmost caution and determine its threat prior to making attempts at neutralization. Arks are to ensure their gear is water-repellant and stand by for deployment when directed to do so by intercom communications.



A Blackened Kingdom


Duration: 30 Minutes (Repeatable)


Falspawn-induced corruption seems to have taken on a new form in the environment, which has left large swaths of Daybreak Province on Harukotan inexplicably blackened. This curious detail is also accompanied by elevated F-factor readings, indicating the presence of Falspawn and the possibility of corrupted Nightfallers. Operatives are to equip themselves for an unknown situation and report for duty to the Gate Area. Orders from HQ call for the investigation and curtailment of these abnormalities, if possible.


Fleet Oracle is blessed with the bravery and determination of ARKS Operatives, and the Falspawn shall tremble before their combined might! See you in the Lobby, operatives!
