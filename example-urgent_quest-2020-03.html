UQs/Concerts: March 2020

The Open Beta Test is here, Arks! Gather your fellow operatives and embark on a never-ending adventure fighting off the Falspawn! Read this article to learn about what’s in store for March 2020!

Welcome to the Phantasy Star Online 2 Open Beta Test on Xbox One! In PSO2 you will have the chance to participate in challenging missions that happen at specific times throughout the day (and at unexpected times, but you won’t find information about those here!)

 

Join forces with up to eleven other players and battle the strongest of the Falspawn in these unforgettable raids! You will know when an Urgent Quest is about to begin when the warning lights and loudspeaker announcements start throughout the ARKS Ship! Make sure to make the necessary preparations before heading to the Quest Counter to start an Urgent Quest!

Urgent Quest “Chaos, Render of Tranquility”

Duration: 30 Minutes. (Repeatable: each run must be completed within 20 minutes.)

Attention all operatives! We have detected an infiltration of Sylvants, Automata, and Draconians, as well as Falspawn, in the City Area of the ARKS Ship. The presence of enemies thought not to associate with Falspawn is troubling as it is possible they are in the early stages of corruption but aren’t displaying the physical characteristics typically associated with the infection. There is no time for investigation, however, and all invaders are being classified as “kill on sight” targets.

 



Urgent Quest “The Grudge of Rampaging Dragons”

Duration: 30 Minutes. (Repeatable: each run must be completed within 20 minutes.)

A den of rampaging dragons has been detected somewhere within the Floating Continent of planet Amduskia. All active ARKS Operatives are to report to the Gate Area, where Gateway Ships are prepared to transport them to the area of investigation. Extreme caution must be exercised upon arrival, as all evidence suggests the presence of Falspawn.

 



Urgent Quest “The Dead Sea Where Dragons Gather”

Duration: 30 Minutes. (Repeatable: each run must be completed within 20 minutes.)

A Chrome Dragon has been detected in the Coastal Region of planet Wopal. Oceanids and Falspawn are present, as well as a perplexing amount of Sylvants and Automata. Early theories as to their presence suggest that they’ve become entangled in the spatial shift caused by the Chrome Dragon. All active operatives are needed for immediate deployment to the planet to gather intelligence and suppress the chaotic situation.

 

That’s it for the Urgent Quest and Concerts breakdown for March 2020. See you in the game!
